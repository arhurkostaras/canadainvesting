Arthur Kostaras

Founder & CEO

Arthur Kostaras is the founder of CanadaInvesting. With a background in financial services and technology, Arthur built CanadaInvesting to solve a simple problem: finding the right financial advisor shouldn't take weeks of research and networking. The platform uses AI matching and verified professional data to connect investors with qualified advisors across Canada.

Why We Exist

The Problem We Solve

Finding qualified financial guidance in Canada is harder than it should be. We built CanadaInvesting to fix that.

Discovery Is Broken

Most Canadians find a financial advisor through a bank branch or a cold call. Neither approach helps you find who is actually the best fit for your financial goals and risk tolerance.

AI Does the Matching

Our algorithm analyses 6 key factors — designations, AUM focus, province, service model, investment philosophy, and availability — to surface advisors who are genuinely right for your situation.

Regulatory Verification

Every advisor in our directory is verified through CIRO, FP Canada, and provincial regulators. No unregistered practitioners, no stale data — real, active, licensed professionals only.

No Commission Conflicts

CanadaInvesting is a technology platform, not a product distributor. We take no commission on AUM. Every advisor maintains full professional independence and all fee arrangements are between advisor and client.
