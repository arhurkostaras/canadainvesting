Key Differentiators

Six Reasons We're Different

These aren't incremental improvements. They're fundamental advantages that no competitor can replicate without rebuilding from scratch.

6-Factor AI Algorithm

Our proprietary matching engine evaluates specialization, portfolio size, geography, fee alignment, capacity, and tier position. Not a simple keyword search. A genuine intelligence layer that gets smarter with every match.

CIRO Registration Verified

Every advisor on the platform is cross-referenced against CIRO's public registry before their profile goes live. CFA, CFP, CIM, FCSI, and PFP designations are independently confirmed with issuing bodies. No self-reported credentials.

Algorithm Position Purchase

Advisors invest in their own visibility. Higher subscription tiers earn higher search placement. This creates a self-selecting quality signal: advisors who invest more in client acquisition are serious about growth and service.

Launch Guarantee

Zero qualified matches in your first 90 days? Full refund. We're confident in our matching engine because we've built it on real data and real regulatory verification. We put our money where our algorithm is.

Direct Founder Access

Enterprise advisors get direct access to our leadership team. Not a support ticket queue. Not a chatbot. A real conversation with professionals who have 15+ years of corporate finance experience across four continents.

0% AUM Commission

We never take a percentage of your assets under management or client revenue. Flat monthly fee. Period. Your success is yours to keep. This alignment means we earn our subscription by continuously delivering value, not by taxing your growth.
